Events

A Bunch of Events in a year

Hue Festivals

It is a big cultural event of Hue city that is held every two years. The first Hue Festival took place in 2000. Hue Festival has become a space of cultural exchange between peoples all over the world. During the festival, there are variety of cultural events taking place such as Hue Poetry Festival, Massive Street Arts Performances, the Dialogue of drums and percussion instruments, Art Exhibitions, the Week of Vietnam's historical films.

Tet Holiday

Tet is one of the most important celebrations in Vietnam. It means Festival of the First Morning of the First Morning. Tet celebrates the arrival of spring calendar. Tet is an occasion for family reunions. Children get red envelopes containing money from their elders.
