Food

A Variety of Food

Bun Bo Hue

Bun Bo Hue is a popular Vietnamese rice noddle dish with sliced beef, pork knuckles. The dish has a mix of spicy. salty, and umami flavors. THe predominant flavor is lemongrass. The noodles are thicker and cylindrical compared to Pho.

Banh Ep

It is made with tapioca flour, pork, eggs, and herbs. This is the most favourite dish in Hue. We can eat it in the morning or the afternoon. I usually have it for my breakfast. It is kind of cheap. It is around 25 cents per each piece.

Banh Nam

The main ingredients are rice flour, minced pork and prawn. We can find in other provinces such Da Nang, Quang Binh, but Hue is considered the place makes this first. It is eaten with fish sauce.
