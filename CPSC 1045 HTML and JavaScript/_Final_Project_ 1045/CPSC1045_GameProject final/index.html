Protect the Earth from the Invading of Other Planets

Score: 0
Lives: 5

Information about the game

- The number of balls of each level is different.

- Each dot has a different color and speed.

- Each dot is worth 100 points

How to play

- To start the game, you will be asked to choose a level that is suitable for you from 1 to 4.

- If your input is not from 1 to 4 or not a letter, you will be asked to input again your value from 1 to 4.

- After you choose your suitable level, click on the ‘Add Balls’ button to set up balls or dots(clicking once is enough).

- After you successfully pass those steps above, you can left click on the “Start Game” button to play.

- You are ready to go.

- After you kill all of them, you can choose other levels to continue playing.

- If you have no lives left, you will be asked if you wan to continue playing.

- If yes, you will be asked to choose a level that is suitable for you from 1 to 4 again. After that, you can start playing

- If no, there will be message 'Thanks for playing!'
