Shooting balls

Score: 0
Lives: 5

Information about the game

- The number of balls of each level is different.

- Also, they have different speeds.

- Each dot is worth 100 points

How to play

- Choose what level you want to play.

- Click on the "Start Game" button to start the game.

- You are ready to go.

- After you kill all of them, you can choose other levels to continue playing.
